How well are countries doing at supporting wellbeing for people and the planet?

The SSPI measures the strength of national policies across 57 policy indicators, offering a comprehensive and data-driven look at policy around the world.

The SSPI organizes indicators into categories, each of measures the performance of a package of related policies in a specific sector. Categories are grouped into pillars corresponding to the three core tasks of government in the twenty-first century.

Three Pillars of the SSPI

The three pillars of the SSPI correspond to the three major policy challenges facing governments around the world.
  • Protecting the natual environment from degredation to guarantee a liveable and thriving planet for all creatures
  • Structuring free and fair markets that promote general prosperity and equality
  • Providing public goods that all citizens share
