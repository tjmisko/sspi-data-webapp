Policies supporting wellbeing for people and the planet are well known. To what extent are countries implementing them?

The SSPI measures the strength of national policies across 57 policy indicators, offering a comprehensive and data-driven evaluation of policies across 66 countries.

The SSPI organizes indicators into categories, each of measures the performance of a package of related policies in a specific sector. Categories are grouped into pillars corresponding to the three core tasks of government in the twenty-first century.
